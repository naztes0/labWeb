How to order?

To place an order, simply browse our catalog, add your favorite vinyl records or audio equipment to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely online and receive a confirmation email with the order details.

What are the delivery terms?

We offer standard and express delivery options. Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days and delivered within 3–7 days depending on your location. Free shipping is available for orders over 5000 UAH.

What should I do if the item is damaged during delivery?

If your order arrives damaged, please contact our support team within 48 hours. Send us photos of the damaged item and the packaging, and we’ll offer a replacement or refund depending on the situation.
